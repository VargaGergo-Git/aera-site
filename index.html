Privacy Policy

Summary: Aera reads health data from Apple Health with your permission, processes it on your device, and keeps the results on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

What Aera accesses

  • Apple Health data (read-only). Aera reads sleep, heart rate, heart rate variability, resting and walking heart rate, VO₂ max, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, steps, active and basal energy, walking and running distance, cycling and swimming distance, flights climbed, stand and exercise time, walking and stair speed, walking asymmetry and steadiness, running dynamics, time in daylight, workouts, workout routes, mindful sessions, and date of birth. Aera writes only completed breathing sessions back to Apple Health as mindful minutes. Read access is granted by you in the Health permission sheet and can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.
  • Photo library (optional). Only used when you choose to save a workout replay video. Aera never reads your existing photos.

What Aera sends to Apple services

Two features rely on Apple-hosted services. Data sent is governed by Apple's privacy terms, not Aera's:

  • WeatherKit. When you complete an outdoor workout with a recorded route, Aera sends the workout's start location and timestamp to Apple's WeatherKit to retrieve historical weather. Apple's WeatherKit privacy terms apply.
  • Maps. When you view a workout route, heatmap, or flyover, MapKit loads map tiles from Apple. Apple's Maps privacy terms apply.

What Aera does not do

  • No user accounts, no login, no profile.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No cloud sync, no backup to any server operated by Aera.
  • No selling or sharing of data. Aera has no servers that could receive it.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. Aera does not see your payment details. Subscription status is verified on-device against Apple's receipt. Apple's App Store privacy terms apply to purchases. For full subscription, cancellation, and refund terms, see Terms of Use below.

Children

Aera is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. There are no accounts, so no personal information is collected regardless of age.

Data retention

All data Aera generates (scores, baselines, logs) is stored in a local database on your device. Deleting the app removes all of it. Aera cannot recover data from a deleted install because it never left your device.

Common questions

  • Aera can't see my health data. Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Aera and make sure all categories are enabled.
  • My subscription didn't activate. Open Aera, go to Settings, tap "Restore Purchases." If it still fails, email us with your Apple ID region so we can check.
  • I want to cancel my subscription. Open iOS Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Aera → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • I want a refund. Refunds for App Store subscriptions are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund request flow.
  • I want to delete my data. Delete the Aera app. All on-device data is removed with it. Apple Health data is unaffected.
